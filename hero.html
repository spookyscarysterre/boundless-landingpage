Boundless

We envision a world where crossing boarders is no longer a barrier to friendship and shared experiences

VALUES

 

To provide connection

At Boundless we value the importance of maintaining relationships and connections, especially for expats who may be far away from their loved ones. We want to facilitate a fun and engaging way for expats to create new connections, for friends to reconnect, and create new shared memories and experiences through our PubQuizes.

Inclusivity

We aim to be inclusive to people of all backgrounds and languages. We want to create a space where everyone can participate and feel welcome, regardless of where they come from or what language they speak. We strive to offer PubQuizes in multiple languages to ensure everyone can join in the fun.

To create high-quality experiences

Boundless is committed to providing high-quality PubQuizes that are both enjoyable and challenging. We want to create an engaging and entertaining experience that participants will remember and want to come back to again and again. We will strive to offer unique and interesting questions that are relevant to an international audience.
