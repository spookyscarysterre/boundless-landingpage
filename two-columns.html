Pub quizes

Boundless is excited to offer a unique and engaging experience for expats - pub quizes! Our team of expert quizmasters have crafted a range of fun and challenging quizes that are designed to bring expats together, reconnecting with their family and friends or making new connections.

Our pub quizes are the perfect opportunity to meet new people, reconnect with your loved ones abroad, test your knowledge, and have a great time in a relaxed and welcoming environment. Whether you're a solo traveler or part of a group, our pub quizes are designed to cater to all levels of expertise and interests.

We believe that a great pub quiz is about more than just answering questions - it's about building connections and creating memories. That's why we go the extra mile to ensure that our quizes are both entertaining and educational, with a range of topics that span everything from history and geography to pop culture and current events.
